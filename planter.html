Planter Box

My Third Woodworking Project

My last woodworking project this year is the planter box. After finishing the barbeque scraper and joineries I have gained knowledge on basic woodworking and how to create and use joineries. So it was time to move on to something harder. For the planter box, I had to create my own design with a limited amount of wood. Thinking about the joinery, the volume, and the shape of the box.
