TIJIO

My name is Tamir Polyakov, I am in grade 9 and I take Mr. Doherty fun tech class This website showcases all my projects from technology class, as well as explanation about machines and tools, my process for each project, and how to create your own woodwork.

Barbeque Scraper

The first project that I have created in tech class was the barbeque scraper. A tool used to clean the barbeque after a good day of grilling. I have created the barbeque scraper from a 2" by 6" by 1" cedar board with woodworking tools, hand tools, and sand paper.

Joineries

The second project that I have created in tech class was the joineries. Joineries are ways to connect pieces of wood together. There are thirteen different joineries and I learned how to create five in this project. The butt joint, mitre joint, dovetail joint, half lap joint, and the mortoise and tenon joint.

Planter Box

My last project this year is the planter box. The planter box is the most difficult project I had thus far because I have to create the design my self with a limited amount of wood, 6-7 board feet.
