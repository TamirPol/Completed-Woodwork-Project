Final Design

The joineries is a fun yet quite hard project. It has taught me different types of joineries and how to create them which has helped me with the next project which was the planter box. One thing I believe I could have done better was the dovetai joints. When I used the rasp to remove wood from the inside, I didn't hold it straight which created curves in both the shape of the dovetail joint and side walls of the joint which created small gaps at the sides when I connected my dovetail joint. I also bled a bit when creating this joint.
